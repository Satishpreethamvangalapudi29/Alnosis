Project Description
AINOSIS overview

AINOSIS is a unified AI-powered digital health ecosystem designed to help villages and underserved communities get instant access to medical services, doctors, labs, and medicine availability in real time.

The platform connects village health workers, primary care centers, doctors, diagnostic laboratories, and medical stores into a single continuum of care, powered by intelligent triage, remote consultations, and real-time inventory awareness.
